About CampusFixIt

Your go-to platform for reporting and tracking campus maintenance issues. We make it easy to keep your campus facilities running smoothly by connecting students directly with maintenance services.

How It Works

1

Report Issue

Submit a maintenance request through our user-friendly form with details and photos of the issue.

2

Quick Assessment

Maintenance staff reviews and prioritizes the request based on urgency and resource availability.

3

Track Progress

Follow the status of your maintenance request.

Key Features

Easy Reporting

Submit maintenance requests quickly from any device with our mobile-friendly interface.

Photo Upload

Attach photos to your maintenance requests for better issue documentation.
